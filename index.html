About Us

We are a team of three Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT) Environmental Technology (EVT) students that have a deep passion for sustainable fashion. Fashion has affected each of our lives differently and when given the opportunity to decide on our end of program project the three of us came together with this value to create Ordinal Wear. This is something we couldn’t have done by ourselves, but with the help of fellow School for Advanced Digital Technology (SADT) students and many faculty at SAIT we were able to bring you this website.
