Founded With a Vision to Challenge the Status Quo

Mission Statement

At Ordinal Wear, we are committed to spreading awareness about the harmful impacts of the fast fashion industry on the environment. Through clothing swaps, educational campaigns, etc. we aim to offer an alternative to the fast fashion cycle and inspire change in the Calgary fashion community.

About Ordinal Wear

Ordinal Wear was founded in 2024 by....Good clothing isn't always easy to come across, which makes fast fashion appealing - after all. It tends to offer a wide range of sizes at low costs. Plenty of people know that fast fashion is bad, but we found that there weren't a lot of resources out there telling exactly why it is at an easy-to-understand level while showing their sources. We also sought to provide more alternatives to fast fashion outside of “just thrift your clothes” or “just shop at X store instead”, as some of us don't have the money to shop at certain stores or have body shapes not often represented in thrift store stock

Our Motivations for Tackling This Problem

1.We aim to shed light on these issues and inspire change in the way we approach clothing consumption.

2.Orar strives to find an alternative to the fast fashion cycle.

3.Every choice we make as consumers has an impact.
